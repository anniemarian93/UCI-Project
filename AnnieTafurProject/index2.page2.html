Succulents

One of the most popular variety of plants nowadays are succulents.There are many types of succulents, some of them are easy to care, so it is a great type of plant to start.
Succulents have thick leaves that are meant to retain water, that's why they won't die easy if you for some reason forget to water them.

Sanseviera

One of the most easy plants that is part of the succulent division is called Sansevieria.It easily adapts itself to wherever you decide to grow them, and what is astounding about this plant is its ability to "clean " the air where it is.
