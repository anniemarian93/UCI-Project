Cheat table for newbies

Some plants are easy to take care of them, and some of them will have you praying that they don't die. But, you don't have to worry, with this table you will have the basic information you need to remind you how frequent you need to water and fertilize your plants depending on the exposure to the Sun. You will see how they react to this schedule and if your plant doesn't like it, it will give you signs that something is not working. With time, you will be an expert in recognizing signs from your plant, but you need patience.

Sun exposure Water Fertilizer
Total Sun 3/4 days 3 months
Partial Sun 6/7 days 4 months
Shadow 15 days 6 months
